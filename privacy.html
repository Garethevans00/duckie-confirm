Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Duckie ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Information We Collect

  • Account information: When you create an account we collect your email address and username.
  • Game data: Scores, coins earned, accessories owned and equipped, and number of games played.
  • Device token: A push notification token to send you game alerts (only if you grant permission).
  • Avatar image: If you choose to upload a profile photo.

How We Use Your Information

  • To operate the game and display your scores on the leaderboard.
  • To send push notifications about daily resets, friend activity, and re-engagement (only with your permission).
  • To process in-app purchases via Apple's payment system.
  • To display ads via Google AdMob, which may use your device's advertising identifier.

Third-Party Services

  • Supabase — database and authentication.
  • RevenueCat — in-app purchase management.
  • Google AdMob — advertising. AdMob may collect data as described in Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Apple Sign In / Google Sign In — optional authentication providers.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We only share data with the third-party services listed above, which is necessary to operate the app.

Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and data by contacting us.

Children's Privacy

Duckie is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us at the address below.

Contact

If you have any questions about this policy, please contact us at hello@duckie-dash.com.
